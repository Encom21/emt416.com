About Elecltriomech and the FAQ's

Q: How often do you meet? A: We meet every week Thursdays 7-Later in the Eveings. at the Site3 CoLabortory swing by and ask for the Electromech Tribe but due to Covid-19 we are taking a hiatus.

Q: What is the focus of the Electromech Tribe and DEFCON Toronto when it comes to Security? A: We have no specific focus. Our meetups usually discuss Web Applications, Reverse Engineering, Mobile Hacking, Automobile Hacking, IOT, and more.

Q: I’m a beginner, can I join ElectroMech Tribe and DEFCON Toronto? A: Yes. Our attendee’s come from every walk of life and profession. it doesn’t matter whether you’re a junior or a senior security professional, you will always learn something new in our meetups.
